UNPARENTING


It started with one promise.

The day our daughter Tharvi was born, we made one promise.

We'll try our best to give her a better childhood than we had.

Not because ours wasn't beautiful. But because every generation gets a chance to improve something.

We wanted her close to nature. Close to curiosity. Close to people. Close to real life.

EMOTIONALLY PHYSICALLY SOCIALLY INTELLECTUALLY

One evening changed everything.

Vriti was shooting reels. She asked,

"What should we call this?"

Without thinking I replied,

"We'll spend our lives learning parenting. But we'll spend just as much time unlearning it."

That became

UNPARENTING.

Not as a business. Not as coaching. Just our own journey.

The three of us.

Why the mountains?

People ask us, "Why Manali?"

Honestly... there wasn't one dramatic reason.

We came. Life became slower. We smiled more. Trusted people more. Spent more time together. Watched Tharvi grow differently.

One day... we realised we didn't want to go back.

Love is the answer.

We had fears too.

The mountains didn't magically remove them. They asked us to trust again — slowly.

Fear

became trust.

Trust became friendship.

Friendship became love.

Children deserve to grow up where love is stronger than fear.

Why are we inviting families?

Not because we're experts. Not because we have all the answers.

Because this environment changed us. Maybe it can give your family something too.

Less rushing. More conversations.
Less noise. More presence.

What is Unparenting?

Not a parenting method. Not rules. Not advice.

Simply becoming intentional. Keeping what feels true. Questioning what doesn't.

Creating a childhood that feels true to your own family.

Slow living, not a schedule.

The point isn't to stay busy. It's to remember what life feels like when nothing is rushing you.

Everything you'd want to ask.

How do we reach Haripur?+

Fly to Bhuntar (Kullu–Manali airport, ~1.5 hrs away), take an overnight Volvo bus from Delhi/Chandigarh to Manali, or drive up. From Manali, Haripur is a short taxi ride across the valley — we'll arrange the pickup.

Is it really baby friendly?+

Yes — we live here with our own toddler. Warm rooms, safe spaces to crawl and wander, simple home food that babies actually eat, and hosts who understand nap times.

What's the weather like?+

Pleasant summers (10–25°C), crisp autumns, snowy winters. We'll tell you exactly what to pack for your dates — including for the little one.

What about meals?+

Breakfast and dinner are included — simple, fresh, home-cooked Himachali and North Indian food. Dietary needs and baby food are easy; just tell us on the call.

Can we work from there?+

Yes. High-speed WiFi, quiet corners with valley views, and power backup. Many families do a workation — mornings at the laptop, evenings by the river.

What if someone falls ill?+

There's basic care in the village, and Manali town — with its pharmacies and hospitals — is a short drive away. If anyone in your family ever needs a doctor, we'll take you there ourselves.

How does the sauna work?+

A wood-fired Scandinavian sauna by the river. We book it privately for your family — sweat it out, then step straight into cold mountain air. We call it a rebirth. It's not for babies, so parents take turns while the other has the little one.

Is there parking?+

Yes — private parking on the property. Bring your own car right up to the house.

Tell us about the riverside.+

There's a private riverside spot for your family — for coffee in the morning, feet in the water, or doing nothing at all. It's yours while you're here, not shared with strangers.

Payments & cancellation, in plain words+

₹10,000 advance by UPI or bank transfer confirms your dates. The rest is due 8 days before you arrive.

The advance isn't refundable, because we host only two families at a time — when you book, those dates are yours and closed to everyone else.

If we ever have to cancel — weather, emergencies, anything — you get 100% back, immediately.

The small print, minus the jargon+

This is our home, not a hotel — treat it like you'd want yours treated. Check-in and check-out are flexible; we'll agree on times that suit your travel.

Custom stays are priced on the call, before anything is paid. No hidden charges, ever.

Anything we haven't covered? Ask us on WhatsApp — a human answers.
